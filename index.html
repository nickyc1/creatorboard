What breaks today

The creator program lives in six places. Nobody knows what is actually next.

Instagram DMs show the conversation, but not who owns it.

Sheets show the roster, but not the latest blocker.

Drive has the raw clips, but not whether ads can run yet.

Meta has spend and access, but not payment context.

YouTube and Google creator ads grow into their own second tracker.

Multiple people reply, so work gets doubled, dropped, or guessed.

The V1 shape

A partnerships hub, not another CRM.

CreatorBoard should feel like the sheet Ben already trusts, with the missing operational layer added on top.

Inbox

Every DM has an owner and a next step.

Pull in Instagram creator conversations, assign them to the right person, and flag what still needs a reply.

Creator

One record for the whole partnership.

Handle, niche, code, size, shipping, PayPal, access status, uploaded assets, and notes stay beside the thread.

Ads

Ad instructions and performance stay attached.

See what to run, where the raw files live, current spend, ROAS, impressions, purchases, and payout math.

Offerings to nail first

Positioning

For teams that do not want another tool, but need the work to stop slipping.
